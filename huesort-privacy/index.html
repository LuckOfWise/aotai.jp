Privacy Policy

App: Hue Sort ／ Provider: lilygames ／ Last updated: June 20, 2026

lilygames ("we", "us") sets out below how user information is handled in the mobile app "Hue Sort" (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect information that directly identifies you, such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the following information may be automatically obtained and used:

  • Advertising information: The App displays ads through Google AdMob. To deliver, measure, and prevent fraud in advertising, AdMob may collect the advertising identifier (Advertising ID), approximate location, device information, and similar data.
  • In-app progress: Your puzzle clear status and best scores are stored only on your device and are not transmitted externally.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Displaying and optimizing advertisements
  • Improving app quality and analyzing issues

3. Provision to Third Parties (Advertising)

The App uses the following third-party service. Please review its privacy policy.

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of or reset the advertising identifier from your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and over. For users under the age of 13, we do not serve personalized ads, in accordance with applicable laws and Google Play policies.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Progress data saved in the App exists only on your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the App.

6. Changes

This policy may be revised without prior notice. When revised, the "Last updated" date on this page will be updated.

7. Contact

For inquiries about the App, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@aotai.jp
